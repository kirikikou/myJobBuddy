Getting Started

Welcome to myJobBuddy! Here's how to get started with your job search:

  1. Set up your profile - Fill in your personal details in the Profile section.
  2. Add your favorite companies - Use the Applications section to add companies you're interested in.
  3. Create cover letter templates - Prepare different versions in the Resources section.
  4. Search for jobs - Use the Job Search feature to find opportunities at your target companies.
  5. Track your applications - Keep your Applications list updated with dates and comments.

The Dashboard will automatically show your progress and remind you when to follow up on applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my job applications?

You can track your job applications in the Applications section. Add a new company, set the applied date, and add any relevant comments.

How do reminders work?

Reminders are automatically generated based on your application dates. You'll receive a first reminder after 15 days and a second reminder after 30 days to follow up on your applications.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your data from the Applications page or Settings page. Click on the "Export" button or "Save to JSON File" to download your data.
